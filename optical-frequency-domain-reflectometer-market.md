<p><p>An Optical Frequency Domain Reflectometer (OFDR) is a sophisticated measurement tool used for analyzing optical fibers. It enables high-resolution spatial measurement of fiber optics, offering precise information on the location and nature of faults or defects within the fiber. OFDR operates by sweeping a laser across a range of frequencies, capturing backscattered light to create detailed reflectivity profiles, making it highly effective for applications in telecommunications, aerospace, and industrial monitoring.</p><p>The Optical Frequency Domain Reflectometer Market is anticipated to witness robust growth, with the market expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.3% during the forecast period.
