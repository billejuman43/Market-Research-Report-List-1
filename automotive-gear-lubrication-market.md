<p><p>The automotive gear lubrication market is primarily divided into mineral lubricants and synthetic lubricants. Mineral lubricants are derived from refined crude oil, offering effective protection and performance for standard vehicles at a lower cost. In contrast, synthetic lubricants are chemically engineered to provide superior performance, enhanced thermal stability, and prolonged service life, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Both types play crucial roles in minimizing friction, reducing wear, and ensuring efficient operation of automotive gears across various driving conditions.</p></p>
