<p><ul><li>Based on Restriction Enzyme</li><li>Based on Affinity Enrichment</li><li>Based on Bisulfite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ctDNA methylation market can be categorized into three types. </p><p>1. **Based on Restriction Enzyme**: This method utilizes specific enzymes to cleave DNA at methylated sites, enabling targeted analysis of methylation patterns. </p><p>2. **Based on Affinity Enrichment**: This approach employs techniques that selectively isolate methylated DNA, enhancing sensitivity in detecting ctDNA.</p><p>3. **Based on Bisulfite Treatment**: This method involves treating DNA with bisulfite, converting unmethylated cytosines to uracils, which allows for differentiation and analysis of methylation status through sequencing or quantitative PCR.</p></p>
