<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Differential ADC Driver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-ended</li><li>Pseudo-differential</li><li>Fully-differential</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Differential ADC Driver Market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Single-ended** drivers use a single signal path referenced to ground, simplifying design but offering limited noise rejection.</p><p> </p><p>2. **Pseudo-differential** drivers feature two signal paths, improving noise immunity by using a reference but still relying on a common ground. </p><p>3. **Fully-differential** drivers provide balanced signal paths, maximizing noise rejection, linearity, and dynamic range, making them ideal for high-performance applications. Each type caters to varying application needs, balancing complexity, performance, and cost.</p></p>
