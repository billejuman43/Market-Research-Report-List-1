<p><strong>The Contract Effervescent Tablets Manufacturing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Wet Granulation"</li><li>"Dry Granulation"</li><li>"Direct Compression"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the Contract Effervescent Tablets Manufacturing market, three primary methods are utilized: Wet Granulation, Dry Granulation, and Direct Compression. Wet Granulation involves combining powders with a liquid binder, enhancing uniformity and ensuring tablet cohesion. Dry Granulation, on the other hand, compresses powders directly or uses roll compaction, avoiding moisture use, making it suitable for moisture-sensitive ingredients. Direct Compression is the simplest method, where pre-formed granules are compressed into tablets without additional processes, ensuring efficiency and consistency in production.</p></p>
