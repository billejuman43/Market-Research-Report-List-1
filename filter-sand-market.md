<p><p>The filter sand market encompasses various types, primarily silica sand and zeolite sand. Silica sand is renowned for its high purity and granule size uniformity, making it ideal for water filtration systems and various industrial applications. In contrast, zeolite sand is characterized by its unique molecular structure, providing excellent ion-exchange properties that enhance water purification processes. Both types serve critical roles in environmental management, contributing to cleaner water and improved filtration efficiency in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.</p></p>
