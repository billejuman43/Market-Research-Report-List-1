<p><p>The plastic pill bottle market primarily consists of two types: PET (polyethylene terephthalate) and HDPE (high-density polyethylene). PET bottles are appreciated for their clarity, strength, and barrier properties, making them ideal for products requiring visibility and shelf appeal. In contrast, HDPE bottles are known for their durability, chemical resistance, and lightweight nature, making them suitable for a wide range of pharmaceuticals. Both materials are recyclable, contributing to sustainability in packaging while catering to specific product requirements in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
