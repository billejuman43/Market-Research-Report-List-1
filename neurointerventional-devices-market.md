<p><p>The neurointerventional devices market encompasses various specialized tools for treating neurological conditions. Embolic coils block blood flow to aneurysms, while neurovascular stents (carotid and intracranial) help maintain vessel patency. Intrasaccular devices isolate aneurysms, and neurothrombectomy devices remove clots from vessels. Flow diverters redirect blood flow and reduce aneurysm pressure. Embolic protection devices safeguard vessels during interventions, liquid embolics induce clot formation, balloons assist in dilation, and stent retrievers are used to extract clots from blood vessels.</p></p>
