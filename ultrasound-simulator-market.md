<p><p>The ultrasound simulator market is categorized into VR-based, AR-based, and MR-based simulators. VR-based ultrasound simulators provide immersive training experiences through virtual environments, enhancing procedural skills without real patient interaction. AR-based simulators overlay digital information on real-world scenarios, assisting trainees in learning while interacting with actual ultrasound devices. MR-based ultrasound simulators combine elements of both VR and AR, offering a blended experience that enables realistic simulations with interactive elements, catering to advanced training needs in medical education.</p></p>
