<p><strong>The COVID 19 Antibody Detection Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rapid Diagnostic Test (RDT)</li><li>Enzyme Linked Immunosorbent Assay (ELISA)</li><li>Neutralization Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>COVID-19 antibody detection kits can be categorized into three main types: Rapid Diagnostic Tests (RDT), Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assays (ELISA), and Neutralization Assays. RDTs offer quick results, typically within minutes, making them suitable for point-of-care settings. ELISA tests are more detailed, providing quantitative measurements of antibody levels, often used in laboratories. Neutralization Assays assess the functional ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus, indicating potential immunity, and are crucial for research and vaccine efficacy evaluations.</p></p>
