<p><p>The epilepsy therapeutics market encompasses various treatment modalities to manage seizures. Anti-epileptic drugs (AEDs) are the primary pharmacological approach, helping control seizure frequency and intensity. Dietary supplements may support overall brain health and complement AEDs. Vagal nerve stimulation involves implanting a device that delivers electrical impulses to reduce seizure occurrences. Lastly, surgical therapies, such as resective surgery, are considered for drug-resistant epilepsy, aiming to remove the brain area triggering seizures, significantly improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
