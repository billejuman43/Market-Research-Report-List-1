Dual-head machines can simultaneously handle two coils, enhancing productivity and efficiency for medium-scale applications. Multi-head machines offer the ability to process multiple coils at once, catering to large-scale operations that require high output and reduced downtime. Each type serves varying needs based on production volume and complexity.</p></p>
