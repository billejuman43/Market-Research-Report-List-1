<p><p>The dual fuel engine market comprises two primary types: two-stroke and four-stroke dual fuel engines. Two-stroke dual fuel engines are characterized by a simpler design and higher power-to-weight ratio, making them suitable for marine and heavy-duty applications. In contrast, four-stroke dual fuel engines offer improved fuel efficiency and lower emissions, making them popular in power generation and automotive sectors. Both types utilize a combination of natural gas and diesel, optimizing performance and reducing operational costs while minimizing environmental impact.</p></p>
