<p><p>The scrubber systems market is primarily divided into two types: wet technology and dry technology. Wet scrubbers use liquid solutions, often water, to capture pollutants from industrial exhaust gases, effectively removing particulates and gases through absorption and adsorption. In contrast, dry scrubbers utilize solid or dry reagents to control emissions, relying on chemical reactions to neutralize pollutants. Both technologies cater to varied industrial needs and environmental regulations, offering solutions for air quality management and compliance across different sectors.</p></p>
