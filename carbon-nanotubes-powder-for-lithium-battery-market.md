<p><p>Carbon nanotubes powder for lithium battery applications consists mainly of two types: Single-walled nanotubes (SWNTs) and Multi-walled nanotubes (MWNTs). SWNTs are characterized by their unique cylindrical structure, facilitating high electrical conductivity and energy capacity, making them ideal for enhancing battery performance. In contrast, MWNTs consist of multiple layered tubes, offering greater structural strength and stability, which can improve thermal conductivity and durability in battery systems. Both types play crucial roles in developing advanced lithium battery technologies.</p></p>
