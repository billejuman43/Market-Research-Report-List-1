Non-plasma-derived coagulation factors are synthesized through recombinant technology, offering a safer alternative with reduced risks of infectious diseases. These categories cater to various medical needs, such as treating hemophilia and other bleeding disorders while promoting patient safety and effective treatment outcomes.</p></p>
