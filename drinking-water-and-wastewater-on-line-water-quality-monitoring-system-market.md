<p><p>The Drinking Water and Wastewater On-Line Water Quality Monitoring System market involves advanced technologies for real-time assessment of water quality. The Electrode Method utilizes sensors to measure specific ion concentrations, providing direct and immediate feedback on parameters such as pH, conductivity, and dissolved oxygen. Spectrophotometry, on the other hand, relies on the absorption of light by water samples at various wavelengths to analyze contaminants like heavy metals and organic compounds. Both methods are crucial for ensuring safe and compliant water resources.</p></p>
