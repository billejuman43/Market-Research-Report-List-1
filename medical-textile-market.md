<p><p>The medical textile market comprises three main types: non-woven, knitted, and woven medical textiles. Non-woven medical textiles, made from fibers bonded through chemical, mechanical, or thermal processes, are used for disposable products like face masks and surgical gowns. Knitted medical textiles feature interlocking loops, providing elasticity and comfort, making them ideal for prosthetics and compression garments. Woven medical textiles, created by interlacing threads, offer strength and durability, commonly used in surgical drapes and implants, catering to diverse healthcare applications.</p></p>
