<p><p>The SMT inductors market comprises several types, each suited for specific applications. Laminated inductors feature thin layers to reduce electromagnetic interference, making them ideal for high-frequency circuits. Wound inductors utilize wire coils, offering high inductance and current handling capabilities. Woven inductors employ woven techniques for improved thermal management. Film inductors use thin films for precise inductance values and compact designs. Other inductors include hybrid models and custom designs, catering to diverse electronic needs across various industries.</p></p>
