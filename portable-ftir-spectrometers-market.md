<p><p>The portable FTIR (Fourier Transform Infrared) spectrometers market can be categorized into AC (Alternating Current) and DC (Direct Current) types. AC FTIR spectrometers utilize alternating current signals for more stable and continuous data acquisition, often enhancing sensitivity and resolution. In contrast, DC models operate on direct current, offering simplicity and ease of use for routine applications. Both types serve various industries, including pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, and food safety, catering to different analytical needs and user preferences.</p></p>
