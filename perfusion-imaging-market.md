<p><p>Perfusion imaging refers to techniques used to assess blood flow to tissues and organs, crucial for diagnosing various medical conditions. The market encompasses three primary modalities: </p><p>1. **Computed Tomography (CT Scan)** uses X-ray technology to generate detailed images, providing rapid and accessible perfusion assessment.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)** utilizes magnetic fields and radio waves, offering high-resolution images of blood flow and soft tissues.</p><p>3. **Nuclear Medicine** involves radioactive tracers to visualize perfusion, useful for evaluating cardiac and cerebral blood flow. Each modality has distinct advantages tailored to specific clinical needs.</p></p>
