<p><strong>The Procalcitonin Rapid Test Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Immunochromatographic</li><li>Fluorescence Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Procalcitonin Rapid Test Kit market includes two primary types: Immunochromatographic tests and Fluorescence Immunoassay tests. Immunochromatographic tests use a simple, rapid lateral flow technique to detect procalcitonin levels, providing results within minutes, making them ideal for point-of-care settings. Fluorescence Immunoassay tests, on the other hand, utilize advanced fluorescence detection methods for enhanced sensitivity and specificity. These assays are suitable for laboratory settings, allowing for more detailed analysis and accurate quantification of procalcitonin levels, aiding in diagnosing infections and guiding antibiotic therapy.</p></p>
