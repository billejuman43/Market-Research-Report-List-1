<p><p>The track lighting system market includes three main types: halogen, LED, and incandescent track lighting. LED track lighting is energy-efficient, long-lasting, and available in various color temperatures, appealing to environmentally-conscious consumers. Incandescent track lighting provides a soft, warm glow and is typically favored for its simplicity and warmth, though it consumes more energy than the other options.</p></p>
