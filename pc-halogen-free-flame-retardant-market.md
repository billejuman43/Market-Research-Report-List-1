<p><ul><li>Organic</li><li>Inorganic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PC (polycarbonate) halogen-free flame retardant market is categorized into two main types: organic and inorganic flame retardants. Organic flame retardants typically include compounds like phosphates and intumescent materials that reduce flammability by creating a protective char layer. In contrast, inorganic flame retardants, such as aluminum hydroxide and magnesium hydroxide, work by releasing water vapor when heated, effectively cooling the material and diluting flammable gases. Both types are essential for improving safety in various applications without halogen-related environmental concerns.</p></p>
