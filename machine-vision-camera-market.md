<p><p>The machine vision camera market comprises two main types: PC systems and smart camera systems. PC systems integrate high-performance cameras with external processing units, enabling complex image analysis and enhanced data handling capabilities. They are ideal for applications requiring extensive computational resources. Smart camera systems, on the other hand, feature built-in processors that allow them to perform image processing autonomously. They are compact, easier to deploy, and suitable for simpler tasks, making them popular in various industrial automation applications.</p></p>
