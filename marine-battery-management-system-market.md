<p><p>The marine battery management system (BMS) market primarily consists of two types: centralized and modular. Centralized systems consolidate battery monitoring and management functions into a single unit, providing streamlined control and efficient data processing for multiple batteries. In contrast, modular systems distribute these functions across multiple smaller units, allowing for greater flexibility and scalability. Each type caters to different operational requirements, with centralized systems ideal for compact installations and modular solutions favored for larger, more complex marine applications.</p></p>
