<p><p>Multi-Layer Ceramic Capacitors (MLCCs) are essential electronic components used to store and release electrical energy. The market is divided into high-frequency and low-frequency segments. High-frequency MLCCs are designed for applications such as RF communication and high-speed data transfer, characterized by their low equivalent series resistance (ESR) and high capacitance stability. Conversely, low-frequency MLCCs are utilized in power supplies and decoupling applications, offering larger capacitance values to manage voltage fluctuations effectively, catering to different electronic performance requirements.</p></p>
